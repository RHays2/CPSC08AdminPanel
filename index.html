To create a tour, you must add a title and stops. You may also give a description. Tours are by default admin-only but can also be set available for all-users. Tours must have at least one stop and may have up to 25 stops. To create a stop, you must add a title, location and media. Media is the pictures and videos you want associated with that stop. To create a media asset, you must select the image or video from your computer, then give it a title and description.
